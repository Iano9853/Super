Dewronger Creations

Dewronger Creations is a youth empowerment group that was created on 17th September 2023. Our aim is to empower the youth to realize and improve their talent in sectors such as modelling, modern dancing, and content creation.

RULES FOR DEWRONGER CREATIONS

NB: All members are expected to follow the rules and regulations. Failure to uphold the rules will lead to automatic removal from the group.

  1. Time management.
  2. Respect all members of the group, especially the leaders.
  3. Always take part in group projects.
  4. Always be present for group online meetings on WhatsApp (Wednesdays) and Facebook (Fridays).
  5. Talk out any issue if you have one.
  6. No addition of new members to the group unless verified by any of the group leaders.
  7. Dress neatly.
  8. No drugs are allowed.
  9. No missing out on group activities unless you present a genuine apology to the group secretary.

All members are required to submit a copy of their ID. If a member doesn't have an ID, they are required to bring a copy of their parent's/guardian's ID.

The ID should be submitted to the group's secretary via email or WhatsApp.
